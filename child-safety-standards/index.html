Child Safety Standards

Last updated: January 2026

Lumyn is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward any content or behaviour that exploits, abuses, or endangers children.

We actively work to ensure Lumyn remains a safe environment for all users and complies with applicable child safety laws and Google Play’s Child Safety Standards policy.

Prohibited Content and Behaviour

The following are strictly prohibited on Lumyn:

  • Child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE)
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming, sextortion, or sexual communication involving minors
  • Sexualised content involving minors, whether real or fictional
  • Solicitation, trafficking, or exploitation of children
  • Any attempt to share, request, or distribute CSAM

Violations may result in immediate account suspension, removal of content, and reporting to relevant authorities where required by law.

User Reporting

Lumyn provides mechanisms for users to report content or behaviour that violates our policies, including concerns related to child safety.

Users can:

  • Report content directly within the app
  • Report users or interactions that feel unsafe or inappropriate
  • Email our team directly

Email: lumynteam@gmail.com

Handling of CSAE Reports

If Lumyn becomes aware of content or behaviour involving child sexual abuse or exploitation, we will:

  • Immediately remove the offending content
  • Disable or terminate the offending account
  • Preserve relevant information where required by law
  • Report confirmed CSAM to appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or relevant local authorities

Age Requirements

Lumyn is not designed specifically for children. Users must meet the minimum age requirements defined in our Terms of Service.

Child Safety Contact

For concerns relating to child safety, CSAE, or inappropriate content involving minors, please contact:

lumynteam@gmail.com

Lumyn is committed to continually improving safety systems, moderation practices, and reporting tools to help protect children and maintain a respectful community.
