Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Lumyn is designed to help users build consistent Scripture habits through daily reminders, reading streaks, and community accountability. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to handling your data responsibly.

Information We Collect

To provide Lumyn’s core features, we may collect the following information:

  • Email address and authentication details
  • Username, display name, and profile information
  • Reading activity, streaks, and progress data
  • Social interactions such as followers, likes, and comments
  • Images, profile photos, or reflections you choose to upload
  • Device and usage data for app functionality and stability
  • Push notification tokens for reminders and alerts

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Lumyn. This includes:

  • Providing reading tracking, streaks, and reminders
  • Enabling social and community features
  • Personalising your in-app experience
  • Maintaining app security and preventing abuse
  • Improving performance, reliability, and user experience

We do not sell your personal data and do not use it for advertising purposes.

Public Content

Information you choose to share within Lumyn, including reflections, comments, profile information, and uploaded images, may be visible to other users depending on your account settings and privacy preferences. Please consider what you choose to share publicly.

Tracking

Lumyn does not use third-party advertising trackers and does not track users across other apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Lumyn relies on trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase (authentication, database, and file storage)
  • Expo (app infrastructure and push notifications)
  • Google and Apple (optional sign-in providers)

These services process data only as necessary to provide their functionality and in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data using secure storage, authentication, and access controls. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal, security, or legitimate operational purposes.

Your Choices and Rights

You may update your profile information at any time within the app.

Account Deletion: You may delete your account at any time within the app settings. Upon deletion, your account and associated personal data will be removed or anonymised, except where retention is required for legal or security purposes.

International Users

Lumyn is operated from Australia. By using the app, you understand that your information may be processed and stored in Australia or in other countries where our service providers operate.

Children’s Privacy

Lumyn is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
